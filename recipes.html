Banana Chocolate Cake

Ingredients
(a) Chocolate emulco 1 tbps
Cocoa powder 30g
Hot water 70g

(b) Flour 120g
Baking powder 3g
Sodium bicarbonate 3g
Caster sugar 70g
Salt 1/4tsp

(c) Cooking oil 40g
Egg yolks 4
Fresh milk 30g

(d) Egg whites 4
Caster sugar 100g
Cream of tartar 1/4tsp

(e) Ganache
Whipping cream 210g
Dark chocolate 150g
Butter 30g

Decoration
Whipping cream 200g
Bananas
Cocoa powder 30g
Warm water 60g

Methods
Chocolate Sponge
(1) Combine ingredients (a) and put it aside.
(2) mix well ingredients (b) and add in ingredients (c) to form smooth batter.
(3) Add ingredients (a) on step (1) into the batter on step (2) to form a chocolate batter.
(4) beat ingredients (d) until stiff peaks form.
(5) Gradually fold in 1/2 of egg white form on step(4) into the chocolat batter until it well combined. Add in the rest of egg white and gently mix well.
(6) Pour the batter into a 9" cake tin. Bake it at 180c for 30-35 minutes.

Ganache
(1) Double boil whipping cream.
(2) Add in butter and dark chocolate , stir until well dissolved. Let it cool.
Chocolate Fresh Cream
(1) Whisk whipping cream till creaming, dissolve cocoa powder in warm water, add into the whisked whipping cream.

Garnish
(1) Cut the cake into 3 layers.
(2) Place one layer of cake as base, spread chocolate fresh cream evenly, arrange banana slices on top.
(3) Place second layer of cake, repeat the process (2).
(4) Cover the side and top with chocolate cream. Put it into the freezer for 15 minutes.
(5) Coat the top layer with warm ganache.

Banana Whole Wheat Cake

Ingredients
70g unsalted butter
30g brown sugar
2 eggs
90g whole wheat flour
90g plain flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
200g banana puree

Topping
2 pieces banana
A few brown sugar
A few cinnamon powder

Methods
(1) Whisk butter and brown sugar until smooth.
(2) Add in beaten egg one by one and mix well.
(3) Pour in banana puree and mix well.
(4) Sift in whole wheat flour, plain flour, baking powder, baking soda and mix well.
(5) Pour the mxture into the baking mould and place banana slices on top.
(6) Sprinkle some brown sugar and some cinnamon powder on top.
(7) Bake for 5 minutes at 180c.


Blackforest Cake

Ingredients
(a) 4 egg yolks
20g sugar

(b) 4 egg whites

(c) 100g sugar

(d) 95g flour
15g cocoa powder

(e) 70g melted butter(hot)

Method
1) Whip (a) till light and fluffy.
2) whip (b) till soft peaks form and then add (c) few times and continue whipping untill stiff.
3) Mix (a) and (b).
4) Sieve in (d) and mix well.
5) Fold in (e) and mix well.
6) Bake at 190c for 15 minutes. remove the cake from the pan immediately when done.

30g milk
150g whipped cream
Pitted cheery filling OR

Some chocolate shavings
Apricot syrup

Method

Carrot Cheesecake

Ingredients
(a) eggs
250 brown sugar
1 tsp ovalette

(b) 300g plain flour
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon powder
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda

(c) 300g carrots(grated into shreds)
100g chopped walnuts
60g raisins

(d) 150g corn oil
Cheese Cream:(mix till smooth)
200g cream cheese
40g butter
40g icing sugar
1 tbsp lemon juice

Methods
(1) Place ingredient (a) into a mixing bowl. Beat till light and fluffy.
(2) Add in ingredient (b) and mix well.
(3) Add in ingredient (c) and mix well.
(4) Add in ingredient (d) and mix well.
(5) Pour batter in a 9" x 9" cake tin and bake at 130c for 40-45 minutes till done.
(6) Invert baked cake on a wire rack.
(7) Cool completely before spreading cake with the chilled cheese cream.

Cheddar Cheese Bread

Ingredients
(a) 500g high protein flour
10g instant yeast

(b) 60g caster sugar
9g salt
25g milk powder
1 egg
250g water

(c) 75g butter
Topping
100g cheddar cheese
5g parsley flakes

For glazing
1 egg(beaten)

Methods (Breadmaker)
(1) Add ingredients (a), (b) & (c) into breadmaker machine.
(2) Select bread dough function (1 hrs 20 minutes).
(3) Divide dough into 60g each and roll into round shape.
(4) Cover and leave it to proof for 40 minutes.
(5) Egg wash, sprinkle cheese and parsley on top.
(6) Bake in preheated oven at 190c for 15 minutes till done.

(b)
100g Cornoil

150g Dark Chocolate button

Methods

Pandan Kaya Cake

Chiffon Cake Ingredients
(a) 150g superfine flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
80g castor sugar
1/4 tsp salt
10g milk powder

(b) 60g cooking oil
4 egg yolks
50g water or (50g pandan juice + coconut milk)
1/2 tsp pandan paste

(c) 4 egg whites
100g castor sugar
1/4 tsp cream of tartar

Method
1) Mix well all ingredient (A) into a mixing bowl.
2) Add in ingredient(B) and mix well to form a smooth batter. Set it aside.
3) Whisk egg whites in ingredient(C) till soft peaks appear, then gradually add castor sugar and cream of tartar and continue to whip till stiff.
4) Fold 1/4 of egg white mixture into the batter and mix well, then pour in the rest of egg white mixture into the batter and stir gently.
5)Pour batter into a 9" round cake tin or 8" square cake tin.
6)Bake at 180c for 30-35 minutes. Remove from oven, invert the cake on a wire rack until cooled. Remove from the mould.

Pandan Kaya
900 ml coconut milk
100 ml pandan juice
10 tbps green pea flour
1 tbsp instant jelly powder
1/2 tsp salt
140g castor sugar
a little pandan paste

Method
Put all ingredients into a pot and double boil it by using medium heat. Stir and cook till mixture has tickened. Assemble the pandan kaya with the cake slices when it is still warm.

To assemble
1) slice the cake into 3 layers. Place 1 slice of cake into cake tin, pour in 1/3 of the pandan kaya mixture, repeat the process.
2) Leave aside to cool before refrigerate 4 hours or over night before removing the cake ring.

Sweetcorn Sausage Cheese Bread

Ingredients
(a) 500g high protein flour
10g instant yeast

(b) 60g caster sugar
1/2 tsp salt
25g milk powder
1 egg
250g water

(c) 75g butter

Filling
150g sweet corn
3 pcs ham
100g shredded cheese
75g mayonnaise
5g parsley

Method
(1) Cut the ham into small pieces, combine sweet corn, shredded cheese, parsley and mayonnaise

For glazing
1 egg(beaten)

Method (Breadmaker)
(1) Add ingredients (a), (b) & (c) into breadmaker machine.
(2) Select bread dough function (1 hrs 20 minutes).
(3) Divide dough into 60g each and roll into round shape.
(4) Flatten each dough to expel air, roll out slightly into a circle and wrap in the filling.
(5) Cover and leave it to proof for 40 minutes.
(6) Bake in preheated oven at 190c for 15 minutes till done.
